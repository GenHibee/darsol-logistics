About Darsol Logistics

At Darsol Logistics, we pride ourselves on delivering excellence through innovation, integrity, and efficiency. As a fully registered company, we specialize in Visa Processing, Business Development, and Business Support Services, providing tailored solutions that empower individuals and organizations to reach their full potential.

Commitment

Our commitment is to simplify complex processes and offer end-to-end support that ensures seamless execution and measurable results. Whether assisting with international travel documentation, driving business growth strategies, or offering operational support, our experienced team is dedicated to exceeding client expectations with professionalism and precision.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and innovative logistics, business, and travel support solutions that connect people and empower businesses to succeed globally.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted logistics and travel partner, bridging distances through innovation and integrity.

Meet Our CEO

Ogunleye Shola Saheed — Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Darsol Logistics. With over two decades of experience in travel and tour management, He leads the company with innovation, integrity, and a passion for connecting businesses globally. Over time, he has personally successfully helped over 5000+ secure a Visa to different country around the World. Including Student Visa, Visitor and Tourist visa, Work Visa and even permanent resident.
